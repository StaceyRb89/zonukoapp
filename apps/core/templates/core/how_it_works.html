How Zonuko works (for parents)

Hands-on projects. Minimal screen time. Parent-controlled sharing.

Three simple steps

Every week is designed to be calm, focused, and doable for families.

1. Choose

Select a small number of age-appropriate projects based on interests.

2. Make

Kids work offline using everyday materials, with mistakes encouraged.

3. Share safely

Uploads happen only after parent approval and save to a private portfolio.

Safety first

Built-in guardrails keep every interaction supportive and age-appropriate.

  • No direct messaging
  • No public comments or follower counts
  • Parent-controlled accounts
  • Avatars and usernames only
  • Admin moderation before showcasing
  • Separate age-appropriate community spaces

What you can see as a parent

Stay informed without hovering.

Projects attempted and child reflections.

Skills portfolio history over time.

Optional printable portfolios for offline records.

Full visibility and control over sharing.

What Zonuko is not

  • Not tutoring or exam prep
  • Not open social networking

Zonuko is a calm, project-based space for independent creators.
