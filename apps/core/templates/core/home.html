Do I need special materials or tools?

No! Projects use everyday household items: paper, cardboard, tape, string, recycled packaging, and basic craft supplies. Supply lists are provided with each project so you can gather what you need ahead of time.

How much time does each project take?

Imaginauts (6-10): 30-45 minutes
Navigators (11-13): 60-90 minutes
Trailblazers (14-16): 1-2 hours

Projects can be paused and resumed, so they fit around your schedule.

Is it really safe for children?

Yes. Parents control all account settings. There's no direct messaging, no public comments, and all sharing is moderated by our team. Children use avatars and usernames only‚Äîno personal information is visible. You can disable sharing entirely if you prefer.
